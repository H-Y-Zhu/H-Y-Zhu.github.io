Biography

Haiyue Zhu is a Senior Scientist with A*STAR’s Singapore Institute of Manufacturing Technology (SIMTech). His research interests include autonomous robotics, embodied AI, computer vision and perception, mechatronics, etc. He received a B.Eng. degree in automation from the School of Electrical Engineering and Automation and a B.Mgt. degree in business administration from the College of Management and Economics, Tianjin University, China. He subsequently received M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National University of Singapore.
